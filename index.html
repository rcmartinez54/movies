The One-Hundred Movies You Should Watch

"In no particular order, these are the 100 movies that you should watch. I left out some major blockbusters and cult-classics because they need no help. Check these out and I hope I at least turned you onto something you've never seen and thouroughly enjoy!"

  • In the beginning of this movie the quote by Sir Edmund Burke is displayed, it reads: "The only thing necessary for the triumph of evil is for good men to do nothing". This movie embodies that.

    A group of Navy SEALs, led by Bruce Willis, are ordered to save ONE woman but their heroism has other plans.

  • John Wick

    Are you afraid of the Boogeyman? If so, you call John Wick!

    A man on a mission after giving up the life of missions (being a hitman). This is one of the best action thrillers of our generation. I overlooked this one because there wasn't much fanfare to begin with but boy was I wrong! I watched this movie and immediately began putting out the word. Not one person was disappointed with my recommendation.

  • John Wick 2

    He is at it again! This time it is because everyone else it coming at him. It is open season on John Wick and he is in a fight to stay alive. Once again, you cannot go wrong with this one!

  • Kung Fu Hustle

    Don't let the name fool you, this is a comedy movie! Set in the 1940's, an aspiring gang member in Shanghai deperately wants to be a part of the most notorious gang in town, The Axe Gang!

    Things transpire and puts him on a path he is not expecting!

  • Warrior

    If you are into Mixed Martial Arts as well as great movies, this is the movie to watch.

    Two brothers become estranged after a tough upbringing due to an alcoholic father. The youngest returns home to have his father train him for an MMA competition only to come face to face with his older brother.
